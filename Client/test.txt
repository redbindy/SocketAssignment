# Bing AI Introduction

Bing AI is an innovative search engine powered by artificial intelligence. It offers features such as natural language input, image creation, summarization, and prompts to creatively display search results.

## Usage Restrictions

Bing Chat is not available if harmful content blocking settings are set to strict. Users must adjust their settings to access the chat feature.

## Jeju National University

Search results related to Jeju National University are private and can only be viewed by the user. The university may have specific content that is tailored for individual users.
